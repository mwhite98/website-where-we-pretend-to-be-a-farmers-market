Welcome to the Haney Space Farmer's Market!

Market Rules

  • Bring a reusable bag – you’ll find plenty of delicious local goodies to take home
  • Reduce waste – use recycling and compost stations where available
  • Be respectful – our markets are welcoming spaces for everyone
  • Use designated bike paths – Haney Space has an extensive network of bike routes. Follow cycling paths and help keep everyone safe while exploring the city
  • Take your time – chat with vendors, ask questions, and learn where our local food comes from
